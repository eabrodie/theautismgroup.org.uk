Special Interest Groups

Many young people on the spectrum have special interests which they would like to share with others, but lack the social skills needed to do so. We can offer opportunities to help make those connections in a structured and understanding environment.

We cater for young people aged between 11 and 25 who have a diagnosis of an autistic spectrum condition and are of mainstream ability, but need support to succeed in social situations. They will be able to bring along a sibling or friend who shares their interest to help encourage social interaction in and out of activity time.

Our staff and volunteers all have experience of supporting young people on the autistic spectrum, are DBS checked and trained in safeguarding. Our groups are held at BCA (Berkshire College of Agriculture), Burchetts Green and in the Nicholson Centre, Maidenhead.
