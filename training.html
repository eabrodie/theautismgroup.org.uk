Training

The Autism Group is passionate about autism education for all and is committed to providing practical, affordable training for parents, carers and professionals. All of our trainers are fully qualified and have many years of experience working in the autism field. We aim to match our trainers to the course content that best reflects their practical and professional experience to give you the best possible information and advice.

Training for parents and carers covers a wide range of topics that promote a positive approach, aimed at boosting confidence and reducing stress and anxiety. All training for parents is provided by trainers with children on the autism spectrum.

Our Courses include:

  • The Autism Tool-kit for parents

    A range of 2 hour workshops each with a unique focus, giving you information and practical ideas for you and your child.

  • The Autism Tool-kit for professionals

    A range of workshops offering information and strategies for autism friendly practice and inclusion in the workplace.

  • Autism in an hour

    A practical overview of autism which is comprehensive, affordable and can fit into a lunch break for staff with limited time and funds.

  • Bespoke packages

    Designed to suit your individual needs.
